About Janken

Janken…

A Culture-Driven Experience


Curiosity is the essence of human nature, and we fulfill our curiosity by digging more into the concepts we want to learn more about, and for us, our interest revolve around the Japanese Culture. The Japanese Culture taught us that everything has a meaning, as they have a concept called “Ikiagi”, which means “having a passion that gives value and joy of life”, the “Ikiagi” concept blew our minds, and we wanted to inject it into our work; to create something of value to our clients, partners, and even for ourselves.


But you might ask, from all names and cultural symbols, why Janken?
Janken “じゃんけん” is a cultural keystone, it is a Japanese term that describes the worldwide famous game “Rock, Scissors, And Paper”. We all know that making decisions can be challenging sometimes, but challenges can be less stressful and more fun if it is within a context of a game! With “Rock, Scissors, Paper” simple mechanics, you can turn a situation into a competitive and fun experience, you can build relationships with people around you, and you can create memories that last for a lifetime. And that’s Janken, that’s who we are!
